MoarToDo: A Free and Simple ToDo List

MoarToDo is a free and simple ToDo list web application that allows you to manage your tasks easily. All your tasks are stored in your browser's local storage, not on our servers. We use Google Analytics for collecting metrics and understanding user behavior. Click the "?" icon again to hide this information.
